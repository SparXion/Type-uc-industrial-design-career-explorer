# UCID | Ice Breaker Questions for Childhood Interests

This list contains 100 ice breaker questions with contextual follow-up questions to initiate organic conversations in the UCID app. They encourage users to share childhood, grade school, and high school interests (toys, media, activities, hobbies, talents) that connect to industrial design skills like ideation, prototyping, and visualization. Questions are grouped by theme and designed for storage in a PostgreSQL database, providing a foundation for the app’s conversational interface before full NLP integration.
